About Tamilnadu Ayyappa Seva Sangam

Tamilnadu Ayyappa Seva Sangam is devoted to the worship of Lord Ayyappa, the Manasaputra of Lord Shiva and Lord Vishnu. He is revered as the Kaliyuga Avathar, blessing devotees with the grace of Brahma, Vishnu, and Maheswara. The sacred Mandala Pooja tradition began in 1982 and has been observed with devotion every year. Formed and registered in 1984, the Sangham continues its mission to serve devotees and build the Shree Ayyappa Temple.
